Eto Misa was a member of Nogizaka46's 1st Generation. She currently pursuing a solo career as a talent and model. She is a regular model for "Bijin Hyakka" magazine.

Trivia

  • Lightstick color: Red and Purple
  • Audition Song: Deatta Koro You ni (Every Little Thing)
  • Skills: Volleyball, Jumprope
  • Worked as a gravure idol before her debut, releasing 1 photobook
    • Winner of Miss Magazine 2011, a gravure idol contest from Weekly Shounen Magazine
  • Former leader of the idol group CHIMO
  • Has an "older sister" image, known for enjoying drinking
  • Known for her singing ability and MC skills, and was known as a strong "utility player" for being able to easily sub for other members when they were absent
  • From September 2017, was appointed as beauty ambassador for the "Beauty Slope Climber (美容の坂道のぼり隊)" segment of "anan" magazine with Keyakizaka46 member Moriya Akane
  • Graduated on March 31, 2019 (Announced February 14, 2019)
  • Married to Olympic Gold Medalist Baseball Player, Genda Sosuke (Saitama Seibu Lions, Japan National Team) on October 2019
    • She was a longtime fan of baseball, even before getting married
