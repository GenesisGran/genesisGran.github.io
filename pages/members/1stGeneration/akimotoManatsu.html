Trivia

  • Audition song: Oh My Darlin' (Nakanomori Band)
  • Favourite member: Watanabe Miyuki (NMB48)
  • Respected member: Suda Akari (SKE48)
  • Oshimen: Ito Nene
  • Favourite singers: Nishino Kana, Alexandros
  • Likes: Animals
  • Interests: Watching sports matches, cooking, cleaning, sewing
  • Specialities: Violin, Cooking
  • Her specialty is baking and she once dreamed of being a pastry chef
    • According to Shiraishi Mai, one of the best pastries she made was gâteau au chocolat
    • Was part of the baking club in her middle school, along with Nishikawa Nanami (Nogizaka46 Kenkyuusei)
  • Has a younger brother
  • Leader of Manatsu Respect Corps
  • Known as the "burikko (ぶりっ子)" or cutesy-clumsy girl persona of the group
    • According to her, she's been called burikko since elementary school
    • Also called "Azatoi (あざとい)" or cunningly flirty
    • Because of this, she's known as being one of the strongest members at Handshake events
  • Her signature move is "Zukkyun! (ずっきゅん)" where she poses with both hands together to imitate a gun and "shoots" the other person while saying "Zukkyun to your heart! (○○さんのハートにずっきゅん！)" with a wink
  • Studied in an all girls university (Ferris University), majoring in English literature
  • Was on hiatus from September 2011 to October 2012 for academic reasons
    • She went on hiatus immediately after being selected for the group. Therefore, she did not participate in any single until Seifuku no Mannequin
  • Attended the same high school as Nishikawa Nanami (Nogizaka46 Kenkyuusei) and Yada Risako (Nogizaka46 Kenkyuusei). All three were in the calligraphy (shodo) club
  • Known as the most non-athletic member of Nogizaka46
  • Skydived on episode 74 of Nogizakatte, Doko? in order to promote the single Kimi no Na wa Kibou
  • Participated in the Japan Expo in Paris on July 4, 2014, with Ikoma Rina, Fukagawa Mai, Sakurai Reika, Matsumura Sayuri, Hashimoto Nanami, Wakatsuki Yumi and Nishino Nanase
  • Shares her birthday with Shiraishi Mai, which they usually celebrate together
    • The two members get along well, with her burikko persona bringing out Shiraishi's frowning "Kuroishi-san (黒石さん)" persona
  • Known for her complex with her big head, contrasting with Saito Asuka's small head
  • Also known for her bad singing
  • She was appointed as Nogizaka46 Captain to replace Sakurai Reika since September, 2019
